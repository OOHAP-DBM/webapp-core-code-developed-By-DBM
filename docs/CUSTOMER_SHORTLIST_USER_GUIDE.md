## â“ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### **Q: Is there a limit to how many hoardings I can shortlist?**
**A:** No limit! Add as many as you want.

### **Q: Will vendors know I shortlisted their hoarding?**
**A:** No, shortlists are private. Vendors only see enquiries and bookings.

### **Q: Do shortlisted items expire?**
**A:** No, items stay in your shortlist until you remove them.

### **Q: Can I shortlist hoardings that are currently booked?**
**A:** Yes! You can shortlist any hoarding and get notified when it becomes available.

### **Q: What happens if a shortlisted hoarding is deleted?**
**A:** It will automatically be removed from your shortlist.

### **Q: Can I export my shortlist?**
**A:** PDF export feature coming soon! For now, you can take screenshots.

### **Q: Does shortlisting affect price or availability?**
**A:** No, it's just a personal bookmark. Prices and availability remain the same.
