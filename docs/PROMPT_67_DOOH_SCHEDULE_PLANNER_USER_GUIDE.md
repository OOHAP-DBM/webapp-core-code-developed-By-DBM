## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**  
A: Minimum 1 day, but we recommend 3-7 days for better availability. Peak seasons may require 2-4 weeks advance booking.

**Q: Can I edit a schedule after submission?**  
A: No, schedules cannot be edited after submission. You'll need to cancel and create a new one. Cancellation before activation is free.

**Q: What happens if my creative is rejected?**  
A: You'll receive detailed notes explaining the rejection. Make the suggested changes and resubmit. Common issues: quality, resolution, content policy.

**Q: How long does approval take?**  
A: Creative approval: 24-48 hours. Schedule approval: 24-48 hours. Total process: 2-4 business days.

**Q: Can I schedule multiple creatives on the same screen?**  
A: Yes, if availability allows. Each creative needs a separate schedule. They can run simultaneously if capacity permits.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What video format works best?**  
A: MP4 with H.264 codec is recommended. Ensure 1920x1080 resolution and 24-30 fps.

**Q: My video is 2 minutes long. Can I upload it?**  
A: No, maximum duration is 60 seconds. Edit your video to 30-45 seconds for best results.

**Q: Can I upload a vertical (9:16) video?**  
A: Depends on screen orientation. Check screen specifications. Most screens are 16:9 landscape.

**Q: File size limit?**  
A: 500MB maximum. Compress larger files using video editing software.

**Q: Do you support animated GIFs?**  
A: Yes, but ensure they meet resolution requirements and file size limits.

### Scheduling Questions

**Q: What are "displays per hour"?**  
A: How many times your ad shows in one hour. 12 displays/hour = every 5 minutes.

**Q: Can I target specific days of the week?**  
A: Yes, use the "Active Days" feature to select specific weekdays.

**Q: What if someone else books the same time slot?**  
A: First-come, first-served. Use availability checker before finalizing. System prevents double-booking.

**Q: Can I pause my schedule temporarily?**  
A: Contact support. Admin can pause schedules. No charges during paused periods.

**Q: What if my schedule shows conflicts?**  
A: Try different time slots or dates. Peak hours fill quickly. Consider off-peak times for guaranteed availability.

### Billing Questions

**Q: When do I pay?**  
A: After schedule approval, before activation. Payment deadline provided in approval email.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and bank transfer. Details in invoice.

**Q: Do you provide GST invoices?**  
A: Yes, GST invoice emailed after payment. Ensure your GST details are updated in profile.

**Q: Refund policy?**  
A: Full refund if cancelled before activation. Prorated refund during campaign based on days remaining. Processing fee may apply.

**Q: Volume discounts?**  
A: Yes, automatic discounts for 10,000+ displays. Contact sales for enterprise pricing.

### Performance Questions

**Q: How do I track performance?**  
A: Dashboard shows real-time statistics: displays completed, completion rate, daily breakdown.

**Q: What if my ad doesn't display the scheduled number of times?**  
A: Contact support immediately. We'll investigate and provide compensation if issue on our end.

**Q: Can I see when exactly my ad displayed?**  
A: Yes, view hourly and daily breakdown in schedule details.

**Q: Completion rate over 100%?**  
A: Bonus displays! Sometimes you get extra displays due to scheduling efficiency. No extra charge.

**Q: Do you track viewer engagement?**  
A: Currently, we track displays only. Engagement metrics (if screen supports) coming soon.

### Support Questions

**Q: How do I contact support?**  
A: Email: support@oohapp.com | Phone: 1800-XXX-XXXX | Live chat in dashboard

**Q: Support hours?**  
A: Monday-Friday 9 AM - 6 PM. Emergency support 24/7 for active campaigns.
